# Employee Attrition & HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most critical challenges faced by organizations today. High employee turnover leads to increased recruitment costs, productivity loss, knowledge gaps, and reduced organizational performance.

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition, workforce demographics, employee engagement, job satisfaction, overtime impact, promotion trends, and retention opportunities using SQL, Excel, and Power BI.

The objective is to help HR teams, business leaders, and workforce planners make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and organizational performance.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to answer questions such as:

* Which departments have the highest attrition?
* Why are employees leaving the company?
* How does overtime impact attrition?
* Does employee satisfaction affect retention?
* Which employee groups are at high risk of leaving?
* What workforce planning strategies can improve retention?

This project addresses these challenges through data analysis and interactive dashboards.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition patterns.
* Measure employee retention performance.
